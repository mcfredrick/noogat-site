Your best ideas always come at the worst times. In the shower. On the road. In the middle of something else. And then they're gone. Noogat is a thought inbox. Say "Hey Siri, I have a Noogat" — no unlocking your screen, no opening an app, no losing your focus. Your idea is captured in seconds and waiting for you when you need it. No folders. No filing system. No maintenance. When you need that idea again, just search. Noogat's AI understands what you meant, not just what you typed — so you can find "that song idea from last Tuesday" weeks later without remembering a single word. FEATURES · Siri-native capture — say it, and it's saved. No shortcuts to configure. · AI auto-tagging — every idea is automatically organized. You do nothing. · Keyword & semantic search — find anything, even if you've forgotten the words. · Tag filtering — browse by topic when you want to explore, not just retrieve. · Edit anywhere — refine ideas directly in the app. · Free to start — core capture and search are always free. NOOGAT PRO Unlock semantic search and bulk export with a Noogat Pro subscription. Your ideas are yours. We will never sell your data or show you ads.
