FAQ

What is Noogat?
Noogat is an app for capturing fleeting thoughts before they disappear. Say "Hey Siri, I have a Noogat" and your idea is saved instantly — no unlocking your phone required. You can also type directly in the iOS app or from any browser at app.noogat.app. Noogat uses AI auto-tagging and semantic search so you can always find your notes again. Tags are color-coded for easy scanning, and you can add your own to keep things organized your way.
Where can I get Noogat?
Noogat is available for iPhone and iPad on the Apple App Store. Noogat is not yet available for Android devices.
Is Noogat free?
Yes! The basic tier of Noogat is completely free to use. If you are looking for more features, you can upgrade to the Pro tier for $4.99/month or $49.99/year.
Are you going to sell my Noogats?
Absolutely not. We will never sell your data to advertisers, nor will we ever push ads on our platform. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
What is MCP? Should I be worried about AI?
MCP (Model Context Protocol) is a standard that lets AI assistants like Claude, Cursor, and Windsurf connect to external tools — including Noogat. If you're a Pro subscriber, you can give your AI assistant read/write access to your noogats so it can capture ideas on your behalf or search your notes while you work. Noogat never sends your notes to any AI provider on its own. AI only touches your data when you explicitly trigger it — either by using Noogat's built-in tagging (which you can turn off) or by connecting an AI assistant you've set up yourself via the MCP integration. You're always in control.
