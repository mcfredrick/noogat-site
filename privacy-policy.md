# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 25, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Noogat ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Noogat app ("App").

---

## Information We Collect

**Account information:** When you sign up, we collect your email address to create and authenticate your account.

**Your ideas:** The text content of ideas you capture through the App, via Siri, or via voice dictation is stored on our servers so it can be synchronized across your sessions and processed by our AI tagging service. When you use voice dictation, your audio is processed by Apple's Speech Recognition framework (on your device or Apple's servers) and only the resulting text transcript is transmitted to or stored by us. We never store or transmit raw audio recordings.

**Usage data:** We collect basic server logs (request timestamps, error codes) for debugging and reliability purposes. We do not use analytics SDKs or ad tracking.

---

## How We Use Your Information

- **To provide the service:** We store your ideas and use AI to automatically tag and categorize them.
- **To authenticate you:** Your email is used only for sending magic-link sign-in codes.
- **To improve reliability:** Server logs are reviewed when diagnosing errors and are not linked to individual users beyond what is technically necessary.

We do not sell your data. We do not use your idea content for training AI models.

---

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---|---|---|
| **Supabase** | Database, authentication | [supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy) |
| **OpenRouter** | AI tagging (model routing) | [openrouter.ai/privacy](https://openrouter.ai/privacy) |
| **Fly.io** | Backend hosting | [fly.io/legal/privacy-policy](https://fly.io/legal/privacy-policy) |
| **Apple Speech Recognition** | Voice dictation transcription | [apple.com/legal/privacy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) |

Your idea content is sent to OpenRouter for AI tagging. OpenRouter routes requests to underlying AI model providers. We do not send your email or account identifier to OpenRouter — only the text of your idea.

When you use voice dictation, audio is processed by Apple's Speech Recognition service subject to Apple's privacy policy. We receive only the transcribed text; raw audio is never sent to our servers.

---

## Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. If you delete your account through the App (Settings → Delete Account), all your ideas and your authentication record are permanently deleted within minutes. This cannot be undone.

---

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data (all your ideas are visible in the App)
- **Export** your data (contact us at the email below)
- **Delete** your data (Settings → Delete Account, or contact us)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under GDPR. Contact us to exercise them.

---

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
