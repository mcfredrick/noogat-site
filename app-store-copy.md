## 3. Description (max 4000 chars)

Say "Hey Siri, I have a Noogat" — and your thought is saved, tagged, and searchable before you finish the sentence.

No app to open. No screen to unlock. Just speak, and Noogat handles the rest.

**Capture anything, from anywhere.**
Whether you're driving, mid-workout, or in the middle of a meeting — Noogat captures your thought the instant you have it. Built on Apple's AppIntents framework, Noogat works directly with Siri. Not a shortcut workaround. Not a third-party trigger. Native Siri integration, the way Apple intended.

**AI that organizes for you.**
Every idea you capture is automatically categorized with colorful, AI-generated tags. No folders to manage, no manual sorting. Noogat reads your idea and figures out where it belongs — so when you need it later, it's already organized.

**Find anything in seconds.**
Full-text search across every idea you've ever saved. The AI tags make it even faster — filter by topic, project, or theme with a single tap. Your ideas are only as useful as your ability to find them. Noogat makes sure you always can.

**Zero friction is the feature.**
Most capture tools require you to open an app, tap a button, wait for a recording to process, then figure out where to file it. Noogat removes every one of those steps. Speak → saved. That's it.

**What you get:**
- Native Siri capture — "Hey Siri, I have a Noogat"
- AI auto-tagging with colorful, searchable labels
- Full-text search across all your ideas
- Clean, retro design that's easy on the eyes (and the brain)
