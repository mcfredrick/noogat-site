Common questions

What's the difference between monthly and annual?

Both unlock the same Pro features. Annual billing works out to $4.17/month — a 17% saving over paying month-to-month.

Can I cancel any time?

Yes. Cancel from iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. Your Pro access continues until the end of the current billing period.

What happens to my noogats if I cancel Pro?

All your noogats stay safe. You'll just lose access to Pro-only features like semantic search and bulk export until you resubscribe.

Is there a free trial?

The Free tier is free forever — no card required. You can try Pro features are available in the app after subscribing via the App Store.
